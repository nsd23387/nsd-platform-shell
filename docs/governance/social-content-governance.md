## Purpose

This document defines the **governance framework** for all social content associated with NSD. It establishes authority, approval workflows, and usage rules to ensure brand consistency, legal compliance, and reputational protection across all social platforms.

### Scope

This governance applies to:

- All social media posts referencing NSD, its products, services, or personnel
- All platforms where NSD maintains an official presence
- All individuals authorized to create or publish content on behalf of NSD
- All AI-assisted content creation involving NSD brand materials

### What This Document Is

| Governance Scope | Description |
|------------------|-------------|
| **Authority Definition** | Who may create and approve social content |
| **Platform Coverage** | Which platforms are governed |

## Non-Negotiable Rules

1. **No Unauthorized Posting:** Content may not be published to any NSD social channel without approval from a designated Content Approver.
2. **No Bypassing Approval:** The approval chain must be followed regardless of urgency. Emergency procedures exist but still require approval.
3. **No Personal Opinions as NSD:** Individuals may not express personal opinions while representing NSD's official channels.
4. **No Unvetted AI Content:** AI-generated or AI-assisted content must be reviewed and approved by a human before publication.
5. **No Competitor Disparagement:** NSD social content may not disparage competitors, their products, or their personnel.
6. **No Confidential Disclosure:** Internal operations, financials, unreleased products, or customer data may never be disclosed via social channels.
7. **No Unauthorized Crisis Response:** Crisis-related communications must follow the Crisis Communication Protocol and require Executive approval.

## Prohibited Content

The following content is **explicitly prohibited** on all NSD social channels:

### Absolutely Prohibited

| Category | Prohibition | Rationale |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| **Confidential Information** | Internal financials, unreleased products, customer data, internal communications | Legal and competitive risk |
| **Competitor Disparagement** | Negative statements about competitors, their products, or personnel | Professionalism and legal risk |
| **Political Content** | Partisan political statements, candidate endorsements, political commentary | Brand neutrality |
| **Religious Content** | Religious statements, endorsements, or commentary | Brand neutrality |
| **Controversial Topics** | Divisive social issues unrelated to NSD business | Reputational risk |
| **Unverified Claims** | Performance claims, statistics, or facts without verification | Legal and credibility risk |
| **Personal Attacks** | Negative statements about individuals, public figures, or groups | Professionalism and legal risk |
| **Discriminatory Content** | Content that discriminates based on protected characteristics | Legal and ethical requirements |
| **Misleading Information** | False or deliberately misleading statements | Legal and credibility risk |
| **Unauthorized Endorsements** | Celebrity or influencer endorsements without proper contracts | Legal and FTC compliance |

### Restricted Content (Requires Special Approval)

| Category | Restriction | Required Approval |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Customer Information** | May only be shared with explicit written consent | Legal + Marketing Director |
| **Employee Information** | May only be shared with explicit written consent | HR + Marketing Director |
| **Financial Information** | May only be shared if publicly disclosed | Legal + Executive |
| **Forward-Looking Statements** | Must include appropriate disclaimers | Legal + Executive |
| **Regulated Claims** | Health, safety, environmental claims require substantiation | Legal + Executive |
| **User-Generated Content** | Must have rights clearance before reposting | Legal + Brand Manager |

### Response Restrictions

| Scenario | Restriction | Required Action |
|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Negative Comments** | Do not engage defensively or argumentatively | Escalate to Communications |
| **Customer Complaints** | Do not discuss specifics publicly | Move to private channel |
| **Legal Threats** | Do not respond | Escalate to Legal immediately |
| **Media Inquiries** | Do not respond on social channels | Redirect to PR contact |
| **Competitor Engagement** | Do not engage in public disputes | Ignore or escalate |

## AI Governance

### Purpose

This section defines the boundaries for AI-assisted content creation for NSD social channels. All AI usage in social content creation must comply with these rules.

### What AI May Assist With

| Permitted Use | Conditions | Human Requirement |
|---------------|------------|-------------------|
| **Brainstorming** | Generating content ideas and themes | Human selects and refines ideas |
| **Draft Generation** | Creating initial draft content | Human must review and edit before approval |
| **Hashtag Research** | Suggesting relevant hashtags | Human validates relevance and appropriateness |
| **Caption Variations** | Generating alternative phrasings | Human selects final version |
| **Scheduling Suggestions** | Recommending optimal posting times | Human makes final decision |
| **Grammar/Spelling** | Proofreading assistance | Human reviews corrections |
| **Translation Assistance** | Translating content to other languages | Native speaker must review |
| **Image Alt Text** | Generating accessibility descriptions | Human reviews for accuracy |
| **Trend Analysis** | Identifying relevant trends | Human decides on participation |
| **Sentiment Analysis** | Analyzing audience reactions | Human interprets and acts |

### What AI Must NEVER Do

| Prohibited Use | Rationale |
|----------------|-----------|
| **Autonomous Posting** | All content must have human approval before publication |
| **Direct Audience Response** | AI may not respond to comments, messages, or mentions without human review |
| **Crisis Communication** | AI may not draft or assist with crisis-related content |
| **Legal/Compliance Content** | AI may not generate content involving legal claims or compliance matters |
| **Final Content Approval** | AI may not serve as an approver in the approval chain |
| **Customer Data Processing** | AI may not process or incorporate customer data into content |
| **Impersonation** | AI may not generate content that impersonates specific individuals |
| **Fact Generation** | AI may not generate statistics, facts, or claims without human verification |
| **Unattributed Content** | AI-generated content must not be presented as human-created thought leadership |
| **Competitor Analysis for Content** | AI may not analyze competitors for the purpose of creating comparative content |

### Human-in-the-Loop Requirements

| Stage | Requirement | Responsible Party |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Content Brief** | Human must define content objectives and constraints before AI assists | Content Creator |
| **Draft Review** | Human must review all AI-generated draft content | Content Creator |
| **Fact Verification** | Human must verify all facts, statistics, and claims in AI-assisted content | Content Creator |
| **Brand Alignment** | Human must ensure AI-assisted content aligns with brand voice and guidelines | Brand Manager |
| **Final Approval** | Human Content Approver must approve before publication | Content Approver |
| **Publication** | Human must initiate publication (no autonomous publishing) | Content Creator |
| **Monitoring** | Human must monitor responses to AI-assisted content | Community Manager |

### AI Disclosure Requirements

| Scenario | Disclosure Requirement |
|----------|----------------------|
| **Substantially AI-Generated Content** | Internal documentation must note AI assistance |
| **AI-Generated Images** | Must be disclosed if platform requires or if deceptive |
| **AI-Assisted Research** | No disclosure required for internal research |
| **AI-Edited Content** | No disclosure required for grammar/style assistance |

### AI Tool Governance

| Requirement | Specification |
|-------------|---------------|
| **Approved Tools Only** | Only AI tools approved by IT Security may be used |
| **No Confidential Input** | Confidential NSD information may not be input into external AI tools |
| **No Customer Data** | Customer data may not be processed by AI tools |
| **Audit Trail** | AI-assisted content must be documented in content management system |
| **Tool Review** | AI tools must be reviewed annually for continued approval |

## Enforcement

### Compliance Monitoring

| Mechanism | Frequency | Owner |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| **Approval Workflow Audit** | Monthly | Marketing Director |
| **Content Review Sampling** | Weekly | Brand Manager |
| **Platform Access Audit** | Quarterly | IT Security |
| **AI Usage Audit** | Quarterly | Marketing Director |
| **Governance Training Verification** | Annually | HR |

### Violation Response

| Violation Severity | Examples | Response |
|--------------------|----------|----------|
| **Minor** | Missed approval step for low-risk content | Coaching, process reminder |
| **Moderate** | Unapproved promotional content, brand guideline deviation | Formal warning, retraining required |
| **Severe** | Confidential disclosure, prohibited content posted, autonomous AI posting | Immediate access revocation, HR involvement |
| **Critical** | Legal violation, major reputational damage | Access revocation, disciplinary action, legal review |

### Remediation

| Violation | Immediate Action | Follow-Up |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------|
| **Unapproved Content** | Remove content immediately | Document incident, retrain |
| **Prohibited Content** | Remove content immediately, escalate to Legal | Incident review, process update |
| **AI Policy Violation** | Remove content, revoke AI tool access | Incident review, retraining |
| **Access Abuse** | Revoke access immediately | HR review, access audit |
